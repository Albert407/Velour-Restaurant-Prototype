Our Story

Crafted with
passion and purpose

Velour was born from a simple conviction: that a meal should be more than sustenance — it should be an event. Our chef, trained in Paris and Tokyo, brings a decade of Michelin-starred experience to Lagos, fusing West African heritage with global technique.

Every ingredient is sourced from trusted local farmers and artisans. Every plate is designed to surprise, comfort, and leave a lasting memory.
